Larkana

Geographical Position:

Larkana district is located in the Division of Larkano, Sindh, Pakistan. Positioned strategically in Sindh, Larkana is the second-largest city on the western bank of the Indus River, following Karachi. It is situated at approximately 27° 33' North latitude and 65° 16' East longitude. The city is located in upper Sindh, approximately 85 km southwest of Sukkur.

History

Larkano was founded about 300 years ago by a tribe of Sindh called "Laraks" on the banks of the Ghaad Waah, which was dug at the behest of the Kalhora rulers. It had fruit and flower gardens along it, and in time, settlers like the sailors from 'Laar' came and settled down here, which got the place christened Larkana.
